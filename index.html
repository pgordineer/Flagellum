Flagellum

How to Play

  • Entry Mode: Type the country name or 2-letter code. Each incorrect guess reduces your possible score by 1/4 point (minimum 0). Using a hint reduces your possible score by 1/2 point. If you use a hint and have already lost 1/2 point or more, you get 0 for that flag. Skipping gives 0 points but counts as an attempt.
  • Multiple Choice: Pick the correct country from 4 options. Each wrong answer reduces your possible score: 1 point for first try, 2/3 for second, 1/3 for third, 0 for last or if all are wrong. Already tried answers are grayed out.
  • High scores are saved in your browser. A new high score is set if you get more points, or the same points in fewer attempts.
  • Click "Back to Menu" to end your current game and save your score.
